Practice Areas

Criminal Law, Every Dimension of It

From bail hearings to trial acquittals to appellate reversals — this chamber handles the full spectrum of criminal litigation with precision and tenacity.

01

Bail & Anticipatory Bail

Urgent bail applications, anticipatory bail under CrPC 438, and bail before trial. Available 24/7 for arrests and custodial emergencies.

02

Criminal Trial Defence

Comprehensive defence in sessions and magistrate courts — from charge framing to arguments on acquittal. IPC, special acts and state laws.

03

NDPS & Drug Cases

Strategic defence in narcotics cases including NDPS Act. Challenging search, seizure, FSL reports and procedural irregularities.

04

White Collar Crime

Fraud, cheating, forgery, cybercrime, economic offences under IPC and special statutes like PMLA, IT Act, and Companies Act.

05

Patna High Court Appeals

Revision petitions, criminal appeals, and writ petitions before the Patna High Court challenging conviction, sentence, or procedure.

06

Quashing of FIR

Filing petitions under Section 528 BNSS to quash FIRs, charge sheets and criminal proceedings before the Patna High Court.
